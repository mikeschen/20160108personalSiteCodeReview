Michael Schennum is software developer specializing in Javascript, CSS, HTML and Java. Previously he was a multi-media journalist at azcentral.com in Phoenix, AZ for 10 years. His job responsibilities there included creating video, photos, social media, web content and articles for the news organization. He has also taught photojournalism at Arizona State University.

He has been a guest speaker several times at University of Arizona and San Francisco State University lecturing on photojournalism, videography and photography.

In his free time Michael is an avid rock climber and hiker.
