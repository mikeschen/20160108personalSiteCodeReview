Michael Schennum is software developer specializing in Javascript, CSS, HTML and Java. Previously he was a multi-media journalist at The Arizona Republic in Phoenix, AZ for 10 years. His job responsiblities included creating video, photos, social media, web content and articles for the news organization. He has also taught photojournalism at Arizona State University. Michael graduated with a BFA from UC Santa Cruz. He is an active member of AAJA, and had sat on the board of the local Arizona chapter for seven years. He has been a guest speaker several times at University of Arizona and San Francisco State University lecturing on photojournalism, videography and photography. In his free time Michael is an avid rock climber and hiker.
